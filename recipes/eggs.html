Eggs and Toast

Description

Nothing starts your day off right like a nice runny egg with a piece of crispy toast.

Ingredients

  • Farm-fresh eggs
  • Sourdough bread
  • Smart Balance
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Put olive oil in pan on medium heat
  2. Put toast in toaster
  3. Once oil is hot, crack three eggs into pan
  4. Let eggs sit until bubbly, then flip
  5. Season eggs
  6. After about a minute, put eggs on plate
  7. Spread Smart Balance on toast
