Pasta

Description

This delicious dish is one of the author's favorites. With al dente penne noodles, a rich cream sauce, and a generous helping of italian seasoning, this is a perfect entree for any event.

Ingredients

  • Penne noodles
  • Arrabiata sauce
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Onion
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil. Salt and add one box of penne noodles. Boil for 7 minutes.
  2. As water boils, heat one pound of Gardein veggie crumbles in a pan with oil. Cook until brown.
  3. Add one jar of arrabiata sauce to pan with ground beef. Simmer and season.
  4. Add handfuls of spinach to sauce, letting it cook down.
  5. Add penne noodles to pan. Stir and simmer.
  6. Let cool and serve.
