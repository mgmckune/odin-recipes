Burger

Description

A classic dish, the cheeseburger never fails to disappoint. Go classic or make your own variations!

Ingredients

  • Beyond Burger Vegetarian Patties
  • Hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Barbecue sauce

Steps

  1. Fry patty til brown
  2. Chop lettuce and tomato
  3. Toast bun
  4. Remove patty, add toppings
